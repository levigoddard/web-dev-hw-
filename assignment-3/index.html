Meatloaf Muffins

Meatloaf Muffins are a quick and easy meal to make.

They are also portable and perfect to send with lunch.

Ingredients

2 Pounds lean ground beef

1, 10.5 ounce can of condensed vegetable soup

1 cup dry bread crumbs

2 eggs

1 teaspoon salt

1 pinch ground pepper

3/4 cup ketchup

Directions
